What you get

Everything in one place

Compete, gamble, level up, and connect with friends — all inside Grid Surge.

🎮
7x7 ARCADE GRID
Drag-drop pieces onto a compact 7x7 grid. Clear full rows and columns — both directions count. Every line clear resets the surge clock.
🔴
THE SURGE
A wall of red rises every 30 seconds. Clear lines to push it back. Run out of moves or let it reach the top — game over.
⚡
POWER-UPS
Freeze the surge for 5, 10, or 30 seconds. Bomb surge rows. Clear a full row or wipe the entire board. Buy them with coins or earn them leveling up.
⭐
20 LEVELS
Earn XP every game. Each level up grants coins or a free power-up. Higher levels require exponentially more XP — only the dedicated reach the top.
🏆
GLOBAL RANKS
One entry per player on the leaderboard. Your best score earns your rank. Compete for the top 20 spots worldwide.
👥
FRIEND SYSTEM
Every player gets a unique 6-char Friend ID. Send requests, view friend stats, track who is climbing the ranks faster than you.

The Casino

Risk it for more power

Bet your in-game coins or power-ups across three casino games. Win big, or lose it all.

🃏

BLACKJACK

Animated card deal. Beat the dealer. Stake coins or wager a power-up for a chance to win double.

🎡

ROULETTE

A live spinning wheel that slows and stops on the winning number. Bet red, black, or a dozen range.

🏇

HORSE RACING

Six horses, different payout odds from 2.5x to 12x. Watch the race live. Your horse either wins or doesn't.
